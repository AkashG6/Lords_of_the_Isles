Getting started

Every new player in the game is given some gold coins is allotted a ‘Home Island’ and is given a ship of the lowest class, which is anchored, at the Home Island. The world follows a different Time System called as the Tick. A Tick in the game world corresponds to 10 minutes of time in the real world. The world has in total 15 different resource types and a given island in the world by default produces only one of these resources, called as the ‘Produced Resource’, after every Tick in the game. A player can perform the following actions in the game: • Buy an island • Explore an island • Buy a ship • Send a ship to any owned or explored island
